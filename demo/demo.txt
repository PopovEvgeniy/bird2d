Quick help

Controls for keyboard and mouse

Arrow keys - Move
Space - Exit from demo
Left mouse button - Exit from demo
F1 - Disable background mirroring
F2 - Do horizontal background mirror
F3 - Do vertical background mirror
F4 - Do both horizontal and vertical background mirrors
F5 - Enable FPS limit
F6 - Disable FPS limit

Controls for the game-pad (Xbox compatible controllers only)

D-pad - Move
A - Exit from demo
